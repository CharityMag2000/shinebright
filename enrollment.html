Shine a Light Enrollment Rates

Shine a Light would love to serve you children's educational needs. While also sharing with them who Christ is and how we should live according to his Gospel. Enrollment rates for our center are based on the age of the children.

Age Full Time - 5 Days Part-time - 2-3 Days
Infants 6 Weeks - 18 Months $300.00 $170.00
Toddlers 18 Months - 2 Years $280.00 $160
Pre-schoolers 3 Year Olds $260.00 $150
Pre-k 4 - 5 Year Olds $240.00 $140

We structure our days similarly in each of our rooms with our daily schedule so that support staff can easily adjust in each room. We focus on centering our teachings, crafts, lessons, playtime, and reading time on Jesus. This looks different each day but we want each child to continue learning about Jesus through all we do at Shine a Light.

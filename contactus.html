Missions statement

We want to be intentional about sharing the Gospel as we enrich their minds academically. We also seek to provide a safe and loving learning enviroment that glorifies the Lord. This means tha we focus on implemnting Jesus Christ into all that we are doing as we give care to all the children. We proclaim Gods glory by loving His children and their families the the sacrificial death and ressurection of Jesus Christ our Lord. We proiritize being a light that shines bright in your childrens lives each day.

Lead Staff at Shine A Light

We Believe in giving the best educational care to equip your children with the knowledge and understanding they need before entering Kindergarden. We focus on a Christ centered learning where we share the Gospel within many activities we do.

Meet Tanya Pinglan Director

Tanya Got her Degree of Early Childhood and Psychology at Michigan State college in 1982. She then went back to get a masters degree and has taken many trainings to qualify her for the Job she is pursuing as the director of Shine a Light Christian Development Center. She has 3 Children and has always strived to put Christ first in her life showing her kids that Jesus is a priority. She loves working with children because she loves to see them grow, learn, and grasp understand about Gods Word.

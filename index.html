About Our Childcare

Shine a Light Child Development Center is a place where we teach based on what the Bible says. We provide a learning enviroment that involves trained teachers, Christ-Centered lesson plans, developmentally appropriate activities based on specific ages, consistant teacher to parent communication, all shaped by the Gospel.

Our Values and Beleifs

We believe that it is important to introduce the Gospel at an early age so that the children are able to prosper and grow in their relatioship with Christ early on. We believe that teaching a ciriculum daily, implementing the Word of God, and building a starting foundation with the children, that the Lord will continue to build on as they grow and learn more about Jesus.

Missions statement

Our programs mission statement is to be intentional about sharing the Gospel as we enrich their minds academically. We also seek to provide a safe and loving learning enviroment that glorifies the Lord. This means that we focus on implemnting Jesus Christ into all that we are doing as we give care to all the children. We proclaim Gods glory by loving His children and their families the the sacrificial death and ressurection of Jesus Christ our Lord. We proiritize being a light that shines bright in your childrens lives each day.
